Cookie Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026  ·  EverLedge is a trading style of Poppin Brands Limited

We keep our cookie use to an absolute minimum. EverLedge does not use advertising or tracking cookies. This page explains what cookies we do use and why.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and keep you logged in between visits.

Cookies we use

EverLedge uses only essential cookies required to operate the service. We do not use advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies.

Cookie Purpose Duration
sb-* (Supabase auth) Keeps you logged in to your EverLedge account. Set by Supabase, our authentication provider. Session / up to 7 days
Stripe cookies Used by Stripe during the checkout process to prevent fraud and ensure payment security. Only present during the payment flow. Session

These cookies are strictly necessary for the service to function. You cannot opt out of them without losing access to your account.

Third-party cookies

We do not load any third-party advertising networks, social media trackers, or analytics services that would set their own cookies on your device.

Stripe may set cookies during the payment process. These are governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.

How to control cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please be aware that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in to EverLedge.

Changes to this policy

If we change the cookies we use, we will update this page. The date at the top shows when it was last revised.
